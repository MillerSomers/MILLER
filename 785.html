OBITUARY:
LUCILLE FERGUSON, age 87, of San Jacinto, California, passed away on Sunday, May 13, 2018, at Bridging Life Board and Care in Hemet, California. Lucille was born Friday, June 13, 1930 in Oklahoma and lived in the Hemet/San Jacinto area for 72 years. She worked at several places in the valley throughout her career, including Gibbel Hardware, Stridivants and the San Jacinto Museum.
In her free time she enjoyed collecting antiques, especially glassware, and her and her husband, Ralph Ferguson, were active members of the Hemet Jeep Club, the Square Dance Club and the Odd Fellow Lodge.

Surviving is sister-in-law, Pauline Cooper; grandsons Levi (Krista) Yearsley, Jedediah (Misty) Yearsley; three great grandchildren and one niece and one nephew. She was preceded in death by her husband, Ralph Ferguson, significant other, Bill Cleary, daughter, Pauline Yearsley, who passed away in 1987, mother, Louella, father, Norris, grandfather, Monroe and brother, James Cooper.

A funeral service will be held at the Miller-Jones Mortuary Chapel, 1501 W. Florida Ave., Hemet, on Monday, May 21 at 12 p.m. A graveside service will follow at the San Jacinto Valley Cemetery, 2555 S. Santa Fe Ave., San Jacinto at 1:30 p.m. A reception will be held after the graveside service at the Miller-Jones Mortuary Ramona Room from 2-4 p.m. Interment will be at the San Jacinto Valley Cemetery, San Jacinto, CA. Online condolences may be made to the family at www.miller-jones.com
