  • Died: Mar 1729, Wiltshire County, England at age 72

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
FAMILY: Great-Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
OCCUPATION: Yeoman
RELIGION: Member of the Church of England
DEATH: His Will was probated on 3 May 1729. His death could not have been in 1739, as listed on some ancestry family trees.
