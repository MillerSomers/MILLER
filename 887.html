Christopher J. SWART

  • Born: Abt 1859, Indiana
  • Marriage (1): Rosa "Rose" Lilly MILLER on 14 Feb 1883 in Elkhart County, Indiana
  • Died: Bef 1930

  General Notes:

PARENTS: Christopher's father was born in Holland and his mother was born in Pennsylvania (1910 & 1920 U.S. Census)
RESIDENCES: Chicago, Illinois (as per the 1910 U.S. Census, the Chris & Rose Swart family lived next door to the Seymour & LeOna Miller family);
Milwaukee, Wisconsin (1920 U.S. Census)
OCCUPATIONS: Farm hand in Girard, Illinois (1880 U.S. Census);
Machine Inspector at printing company (1920 U.S. Census)
DEATH: His wife, Rose Miller-Swart was listed as a widow on the 1930 U.S. Census, so Chris must have died prior to 1930.
