Lord Charles Augustus BENNET 6th Earl of Tankerville

  • Born: 10 Jan 1810, Westminster, London, England
  • Marriage (1): Lady Olivia MONTAGU Countess of Tankerville on 29 Jan 1850 in Kimbolton Castle, Cambridgeshire County, England
  • Died: 18 Dec 1899, Chillingham Cast, Chillingham, Northumberland County, England at age 89

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Titled "Lord Tankerville";
He was Lord Ossulston until 1859 when he succeeded his father in the earldom becoming the 6th Earl of Tankerville;
He was a Member of Parliament, and served as Captain of the Honourable Corps of Gentlemen-at-Arms, and was Lord Steward of the British Household
