  General Notes:

ADOPTION OF CHILDREN: Blanche's first husband was RUSSELL FORCE. Russell died in an accident in December 1911, one month before their daughter Lucille's birth. Blanche's second husband was CLAUDE WITWER who then adopted Blanche's first two children, William and Lucille. (Information provided by Blanche's grandson - Lucille's son - Bill Palfy in 2013.)
