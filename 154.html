Jacob "Frank" Franklin SOMERS Jr.

  • Born: 20 Dec 1848, Alamance County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Mary Alice WHITT on 4 Nov 1873 in Rockingham County, North Carolina
  • Died: 7 Mar 1936, Caswell County, North Carolina at age 87
  • Buried: Camp Springs United Methodist Church Cemetery, Caswell County, NC

  General Notes:

BIRTH YEAR: His Death Certificate listed his year of birth as 1856, which was incorrect as he lived to be 87 years old and died in 1936. Also, his date of birth is shown on his headstone as 1848.
