Ambrose F. MORLEY 1 2 3 4

  • Born: Mar 9, 1841, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage: Loucelia CARTER on Dec 31, 1864 in Buchanan Township, Berrien County, Michigan
  • Died: Jan 28, 1912, Kalamazoo, Kalamazoo County, Michigan at age 70
  • Buried: Jan 28, 1912, Glendora Cemetery, Berrien County, Michigan

  General Notes:

CAUSE OF DEATH: Epilepsy
BURIAL: His Death Certificate incorrectly lists his burial location as Galien. However, Galien Cemetery in Berrien County has no record of any Ambrose Morley (or A.F. Morley) buried there. The headstone for Ambrose and his wife Loucelia is actually located at the Glendora Cemetery in Berrien County.

  Research Notes:
