Lady Ida Louisa BENNET

  • Born: 22 Jun 1857, London, England
  • Marriage (1): Lord John William Maule RAMSEY 13th Earl of Dalhousie on 6 Dec 1877 in Westminster, London, England
  • Died: 24 Nov 1887, Le Havre, France at age 30

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Lady Ida Louisa Bennet was styled as Countess of Dalhousie on 20 July 1880
DEATH: She died of peritonitis (abdominal infection) at the age of 30 in France after returning from a trip to the United States with her husband. Her husband was so distraught that he died of a stroke the next morning at age of 40.
