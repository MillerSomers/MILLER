C. E. PRIOR

  • Marriage: SMITH

   Another name for C. was Mrs. C. E. SMITH.

  General Notes:

MILLER RECORDS: Mrs. C. E. Smith was the grandaughter of John Abram Prior, brother-in-law of Rev. Adam Miller (making her the step grand-niece of Rev. Adam Miller). She was instrumental in compiling a great deal of information on the descendants of Rev. Adam Miller. Richard E. Bartz (great grandson of Rev. Adam Miller) has also spent many years compiling research on the Miller, Bly and Bartz families, and in transcribing the original handwritten documents of Mrs. C. E. Smith.

  Research Notes:

Miller Data (Journal) by C.E. Smith; privately held by Esther (Bly) Hietala, Grand Rapids, Minnesota (with additional material provided by Richard E. Bartz, Falcon Heights, Minnesota)
