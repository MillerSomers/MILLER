Asa CLAPP

  • Born: 6 Feb 1827, Guilford County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Livinia FLACK on 9 Jan 1851 in Mount Pleasant, Cabarrus County, North Carolina
  • Died: 15 May 1907, Pleasant, Warren County, Pennsylvania at age 80
  • Buried: Mount Pleasant United Methodist Church Cemetery, Greensboro, Guilford County, NC

  General Notes:

Son of Rosa Summers and Jacob Clapp. He married Livina Flack on 9 Jan 1851. Per the 1900 census, they had six children and five were still living. The 1860, 1870 and 1880 censuses reflect the five children as Wm A., James M., Sarah C., Caleb A. and Margaret A. The child who died was Jacob L. (1868-1869).
