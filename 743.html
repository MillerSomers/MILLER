Rev. Benjamin MENDENHALL

  • Born: 14 Feb 1662, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England
  • Marriage (1): Ann A. PENNELL on 17 Apr 1689 in Chichester, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 13 Apr 1740, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 78
  • Buried: Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania

   Another name for Benjamin was Benjamin MINALL.

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
FAMILY: Great-Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Father-in-law to John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
FAMILY: Grandfather to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
IMMIGRATION: 1682, England to American Colonies
NAME: Changed name from Mildenhall to Mendenhall after arriving in Pennsylvania
OCCUPATION: Elder of the Concord Meeting. Member of the Provincial Assembly, 1714
RELIGION: Quaker
