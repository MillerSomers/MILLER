  General Notes:

NOTE: As of the 1900 U.S. Census, Sarah & Matthew were living next door to Sarah's brother Oliver and his wife Jane, on State Street, La Grange, Cass County, Michigan.

Sarah married Charles H. KINGSBURY, son of Asa KINGSBURY and Nancy BULLOUGH, on Mar 12, 1851 in home of father, Rev. John Prior Miller. (Charles H. KINGSBURY was born on May 14, 1812 in Needham, Norfolk County, Massachusetts, died on Dec 23, 1876 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan and was buried in Prospect Hill Cemetery, Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan 5.)


Sarah next married James OREN on Oct 4, 1877 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan. (James OREN was born on Jan 29, 1825 in Union, Clinton County, Ohio, died on Feb 22, 1891 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan and was buried in Birch Lake Cemetery, Calvin Center, Cass County, Michigan 5.)


Sarah next married Matthew F. GARVEY on Oct 19, 1892 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan. (Matthew F. GARVEY was born on May 13, 1821 in Ohio and died on Apr 8, 1901 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan.)
