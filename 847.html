Joan STRODE

  • Born: 1634, Mildenhall, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England
  • Marriage (1): Thomas MILDENHALL III
  • Died: 1682-1683, Marridge Hill, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England at age 48

  General Notes:

NOTES: This Joan Strode (born 1634, daughter of William Strode and Joan Barnard) may be an errant entry. She was listed as the wife of Thomas Mildenhall, III (born 1630) on some ancestry records. But upon finding Thomas' Will, it clearly states his wife's name as "Joane". Most other ancestry records suggest that Joane Stroud (born 1631, daughter of Anthony Stroud and Margery Barley) was his wife, not Joan Strode.

Joan married Thomas MILDENHALL III, son of Thomas MILDENHALL Jr. and Anne, on 12 Aug 1649 in Aldbourne, Wiltshire County, England. (Thomas MILDENHALL III was born in 1630 in Mildenhall, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England, died on 30 Jun 1682 in Somerset House, London, England and was buried on 5 Jul 1682.)
