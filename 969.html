Edwin Dutton MENDENHALL

  • Born: 24 Sep 1835, Columbiana County, Ohio
  • Marriage (1): Elizabeth "Lizzie" Jane ALEXANDER on 27 Mar 1856 in New Brighton, Beaver County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 30 Nov 1902, Bourbon Township, Marshall County, Indiana at age 67
  • Buried: IOOF Cemetery, Bourbon, Marshall County, Indiana

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 7th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Brother of Thomas Corwin Mendenhall, Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake
MILITARY: Corporal; Civil War, Company H of the 162nd Reg. of the Ohio Infantry
OCCUPATION: Carriage maker and ran elevator, furniture and undertaking businesses
RELIGION: Quaker
DEATH: Died of paralysis of the brain
