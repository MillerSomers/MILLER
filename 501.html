Ebenezer "Ebb" B. NEWTON

  • Born: 5 May 1838, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Nancy Jane JONES on 8 Aug 1858 in Iron County, Missouri
  • Died: 3 Jan 1912, Henderson, Baxter County, Arkansas at age 73
  • Buried: Wake Cemetery, Baxter County, Arkansas

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Six Times Removed) to Sir Isaac Newton
MILITARY: Missouri Cavalry, Col. Schnabel's Battalion
OCCUPATION: Farmer (1870 & 1880 U.S. Census); homestead 160 acres in White River Township, Marion County, Arkansas in 1889

DISCREPANCIES:
-- His Birth Date is listed as being in 1838 on various U.S. Census, however his gravestone shows his Birth Date in 1832.
-- His Death Date on his gravestone is shown as 3 Jan 1912, however the Arkansas Confederate Pension Records show his Death Date as 4 Jan 1912.
