Moses MENDENHALL 1 2

  • Born: Apr 16, 1694, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage: Alice (Pyle) BOWATER on Jun 18, 1719 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: Nov 9, 1731, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 37
  • Buried: Kennett, Chester County, Pennsylvania

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 3rd Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Brother-in-law to John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
FAMILY: Uncle to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"

RELIGION: Zealous member of the Quaker Kennett Monthly Meeting and appointed Minister 2 Feb 1726

  Research Notes:

Ancestors of Alexandra Catlin Vaut, et al (http://wc.rootsweb.ancestry.com/cgi-bin/igm.cgi?op=GET&db=gregv&id=I26356)
