Lord Charles BENNET 3rd Earl of Tankerville

  • Born: 6 Sep 1716, England
  • Marriage (1): Lady Alicia ASTLEY Countess of Tankerville in 1742 in England
  • Died: 27 Oct 1767 at age 51

   Another name for Charles was Lord Tankerville.

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Titled "Lord Tankerville";
He was Lord Ossulston until 1753 when he succeeded his father in the earldom becoming the 3rd Earl of Tankerville;
A Member of Parliament for Northumberland in 1748
