Rev. Van Rensler MILLER

  • Born: 12 Feb 1842, Jefferson Township, Cass County, Michigan
  • Marriage (1): Mary E. DICKINSON on 12 Feb 1863 in Elkhart, Elkhart County, Indiana
  • Died: 6 Sep 1916, Goshen, Elkhart County, Indiana at age 74

   Cause of his death was Stroke of Apoplexy.

   Other names for Van were Van R, Vanburen, VanRanceler, VanRansalaer, VanRansaler, VanRensalen and Ransellear MILLER.

  General Notes:

NAME DISCREPANCIES: His name was unique and is spelled in various ways on numerous documents and census data. However, his Indiana Death Certificate states his name was "Van Rensler Miller" and his Indiana Marriage Certificate states his name was "Van R. Miller".
OCCUPATIONS: Blacksmith (1880 U.S. Census);
Pastor for the Progressive Brethren denomination for 40 years
RESIDENCES: Jefferson Township, Cass County, Michigan;
Elkhart, Elkhart County, Indiana (1880 U.S. Census) - (moved to Indiana as a young man)
DEATH: Died after suffering a stroke of apoplexy at the age of 74
