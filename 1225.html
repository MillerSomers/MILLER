Hon. Newton WALLOP, 4th Earl of Portsmouth 1

  • Born: Jun 26, 1772, England
  • Died: Jan 9, 1854 at age 81

   Another name for Newton was Hon. Newton FELLOWES.

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Changed his surname to Fellowes in 1794 (after succeeding to the estates of his uncle Arthur Fellowes);
Member of Parliament for Andover from 1802-1820;
Briefly became the 4th Earl of Portsmouth after his brother died in 1853;
He unsuccessfully attempted to have his brother, the Earl, declared insane in 1814 (the Earl was eventually declared insane in 1823 at the request of the Earl's nephew)
FAMILY: Was married twice and bore 7 children
