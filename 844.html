Ann (II) MENDENHALL

  • Born: 22 Sep 1703, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): John BARTRAM on 12 Nov 1729 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 29 Jan 1789, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 85

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 3rd Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Wife of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
FAMILY: Mother of William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"

  Research Notes:

Ancestors of Alexandra Catlin Vaut, et al (http://wc.rootsweb.ancestry.com/cgi-bin/igm.cgi?op=GET&db=gregv&id=I26356)


Ann married John BARTRAM on 12 Nov 1729 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania. (John BARTRAM was born on 23 May 1699 in Darby, Chester County, Pennsylvania and died on 22 Sep 1777 in Kingsessing, Pennsylvania.)
