   Another name for Thomas was Thomas MENDENHALL.

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
FAMILY: Son of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India


Thomas married Anne about 1603 in Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England. (Anne was born about 1580 in Mildenhall, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England, died on 22 Nov 1640 in Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England and was buried in Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England.)
