Maria Veronica CLAPP

  • Born: 15 Dec 1716, Weisenheim am Berg, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany
  • Marriage (1): Philip Carl JAEGER in 1732 in Weisenheim am Berg, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany
  • Died: 1757, Yadkin County, North Carolina at age 41

  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION: Germany to the American Colonies, arrived on the "James Goodwill" at Philadelphia on September 12, 1727, along with her father, brothers and 12 other family members
NAME: Changed her last name to CLAPP upon arriving in Philadelphia, as did the rest of her family
