  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION: Ireland to American Colonies pror to 1737 (daughter's birth)

DATE OF DEATH: There is a discrepancy as to the year of William's death. Most family trees claim he died in 1787, but the plaque in Alamance (Guilford County, NC) that acknowledges his death states he died in 1777.
