Philip MENDENHALL 1

  • Born: 1736, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Mary JAMES on Nov 19, 1757 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (2): Mary (Reece) BAKER on Aug 31, 1778 in Thornbury, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: Jul 23, 1811, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 75
  • Buried: Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 4th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Nephew of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
FAMILY: First-Cousin to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
FAMILY: Great-Uncle of Thomas Corwin Mendenhall, Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake


Philip married Mary JAMES on Nov 19, 1757 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. (Mary JAMES was born on Sep 26, 1737 in West Goshen, Chester County, Pennsylvania.)


Philip next married Mary (Reece) BAKER on Aug 31, 1778 in Thornbury, Chester County, Pennsylvania. (Mary (Reece) BAKER was born about 1738.)
