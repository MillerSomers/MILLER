Bess L. MILLER

  • Born: 30 Nov 1885, South Bend, St. Joseph County, Indiana
  • Marriage (1): Dr. James H. KELSEY M.D. in 1912 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan
  • Died: Abt 15 May 1946, Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan about age 60
  • Buried: Prospect Hill Cemetery, Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan

  General Notes:

NOTES: Bessie was second wife and a great deal younger. Had 10 years of ill heath before her death. Buried in Prospect Hill Cementary. At the age of 2 years family moved to Cassopolis. She suffered much and was tenderly cared for by her husband.
