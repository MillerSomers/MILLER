Lord Charles BENNET, 1st Earl of Tankerville 1

  • Born: 1674, England
  • Marriage: Lady Mary GREY, Countess of Tankerville in 1695
  • Died: May 21, 1722, England at age 48

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Titled "Lord Tankerville";
Son of John Bennet, 1st Baron Ossulston;
He succeeded his father in the barony in 1695 becoming The Lord Ossulston and was able to take a seat in the House of Lords;
In 1714 he was created 1st Earl of Tankerville;
In 1716 he was made a Knight of the Thistle
