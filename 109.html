  General Notes:

RESIDENCES: Elkhart, Elkhart County, Illinois (1880 U.S. Census);
Milwaukee, Wisconsin (1920 U.S. Census)
DEATH: On the 1900 U.S. Census, Mary is not listed with her husband. But it does list her husband Van Rensler (living with their son Owen), whose marital status was "widow". I believe its likely Mary died prior to 1900.

Mary married Rev. Van Rensler MILLER, son of Rev. John Prior MILLER and Mary Jane SHRUM, on Feb 12, 1863 in Elkhart, Elkhart County, Indiana. (Rev. Van Rensler MILLER was born on Feb 12, 1842 in Jefferson Township, Cass County, Michigan and died on Sep 6, 1916 in Goshen, Elkhart County, Indiana.) The cause of his death was Stroke of Apoplexy.
