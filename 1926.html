  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION: Germany to the American Colonies, arrived on the "James Goodwill" at Philadelphia on September 12, 1727, along with his wife's other family members


John married Maria Elizabeth CLAPP, daughter of Johann Jost KLAPP and Anne Margaretha STAUDER, on 29 Oct 1726 in Weisenheim am Berg, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. (Maria Elizabeth CLAPP was born in 1706 in Istha, Hessen, Germany and died after 1732 in Guilford County, North Carolina.)
