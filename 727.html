John married Elizabeth May PARKS, daughter of Wayman Adair "William" PARKS and Rebecca Louisa "Lolly" MENDENHALL, on Sep 1, 1891 in Canadian, Choctaw Land (Pittsburg County), Oklahoma. (Elizabeth May PARKS was born on Jan 25, 1871 in Central City, Sebastian County, Arkansas, died on Apr 2, 1902 in Pittsburg County, Arkansas and was buried in Massey Cemetery, Crowder, Pittsburg County, Oklahoma 7.)


John next married Carrie Anne BOZEMAN, daughter of Jasper Tillman BOZEMAN and Nancy Jane WIGGINS, about 1913 in Mitchell County, Georgia. (Carrie Anne BOZEMAN was born on Jan 29, 1876 in Ridge, Monroe County, Alabama and died on Aug 25, 1916.)


John next married Grace ROSE on Jul 21, 1902 in Choctaw Nation, Indian Territory, Pittsburg County, Oklahoma.


John next married Anna Jane STONE about 1908 in McClain County, Oklahoma. (Anna Jane STONE was born on Mar 16, 1888 in Texas and died on Oct 18, 1951 in Panhandle, Carson County, Texas.)


John next married Barbra Allen in Oklahoma. (Barbra Allen was born about 1876 in Texas.)
