Lucille Angeline (Force) WITWER

  • Born: 11 Jan 1912, Ohio
  • Marriage (1): Donald (Dayton) PHILLIPS
  • Marriage (2): PALFY
  • Died: 5 Jan 1974, Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California at age 61

  General Notes:

ADOPTION: Lucille's birth name was LUCILLE ANGELINE FORCE. Lucille's biological father was Russell Force who died in an accident one month before Lucille's birth. Lucille's stepfather Claude Witwer later adopted Lucille whose name was changed to LUCILLE ANGELINE WITWER. (Information provided by Lucille's son Bill Palfy in 2013.)

DEATH: Lucille's son Bill Palfy claims she died in Los Angeles, and not in Ohio.


Lucille married Donald (Dayton) PHILLIPS.


Lucille next married PALFY.
