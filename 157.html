Mildred Malone SOMERS

  • Born: 26 Sep 1905, Caswell County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Allen Foster HENSEL on 23 Apr 1933 in Guilford County, North Carolina
  • Died: 12 Feb 1988, Fieldale, Henry County, Virginia at age 82
  • Buried: Carolina Memorial Park, Concord, Cabarrus County, North Carolina

  General Notes:

OCCUPATION: Stenographer
RESIDENCE: Friendship Township, Guilford County, NC (1930 U.S. Census)
NOTES:
-- 3rd of 5 children of Robert Lee Somers and wife Margaret Melinda, who was also born a Somers. The family lived in Alamance County, NC in 1910 and 1920, and in Guilford County in 1930.
-- Mildred married Allen Foster Hensel. They were living in Charlotte by 1940. They had 2 children by 1940: Robert Allen Hensel and Norma Magaret Hensel.
-- Mildred & Foster's 2 children died within 2 months of each other in 1968. Then her husband Foster died 6 months later.
-- Resident of Henry County, VA at the time of her death.
