  General Notes:

NOTES:
--Son of Amos & Elizabeth Eller.
--Occupation: Miller in flour mill in 1860, 1870, 1880. He was farming in 1900.
--He married Sophira Lowder on Feb. 15, 1855 in Rowan County. They had 6 children (names in wife's memorial).
--Confederate Veteran; he enlisted in September 1861 and served as Private in Company D, 34th NC Infantry.
