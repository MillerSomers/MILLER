Jacob SUMMERS 1 2

  • Born: 1710, Ober Bexbach, Germany
  • Marriage: Anna Margaret FAUST in 1751 in Ober Bexbach, Germany
  • Died: 1798, Guilford County, North Carolina at age 88
  • Buried: Friedens Lutheran Church Cemetery, Gibsonville, Guilford County, NC 3

   Other names for Jacob were Hans Jacob SUMER and John Jacob SUMMER.

  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION: Germany to America, arriving at Port of Philadelphia in 1751 on the ship "Neptune". Jacob and Margaret then journeyed to Guilford County, North Carolina by wagon the following spring. Jacob was one of the founders of old Friedens Lutheran Church and was active in its work, as was his son, Capt. Peter Summers.
