Rebecca Louisa "Lolly" MENDENHALL 1 2

  • Born: Feb 3, 1837, Cleveland County, North Carolina
  • Marriage: Wayman Adair "William" PARKS in 1858
  • Died: Nov 12, 1899, Pittsburg County, Arkansas at age 62

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 7th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
ANCESTRY: Great-Great-Niece of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Thrice Removed) to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
ANCESTRY: Third-Cousin to Thomas Corwin Mendenhall (born 1841), Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake
