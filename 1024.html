Ruth GILPIN

  • Born: 28 Aug 1697, Birmingham Township, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Joseph MENDENHALL on 30 Oct 1718 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 1760, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 63

  General Notes:

FAMILY: Ruth was the daughter of Joseph and Hannah (Glover) Gilpin. She, along with 14 siblings, was born in a cave on the east side of Brandywine Creek, about a mile south of Dilworthtown. The cave served as the first dwelling place of her parents.
FAMILY: Sister-in-law to John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
FAMILY: Aunt to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"

  Research Notes:

Mendenhall Family Association (http://mendenhall.org/mfa/tng/getperson.php?personID=I25408&tree=Mendenhall2010)


Ruth married Joseph MENDENHALL, son of Rev. Benjamin MENDENHALL and Ann A. PENNELL, on 30 Oct 1718 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania. (Joseph MENDENHALL was born on 17 May 1692 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania, died on 13 May 1748 in Pennsbury, Chester County, Pennsylvania and was buried in Pennsbury, Chester County, Pennsylvania.)
