  • Born: Abt 1694, Manor Gore, Donegal of Gore Mount, Ireland
  • Marriage (1): Unknown

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Settled in County Antrim in Ireland during the reign of Queen Anne (sometime between 1702 and 1714) and became Baronet of Manor Gore. (A Baronet is the holder of a hereditary baronetcy awarded by the British Crown.)

ADDENDUM: Unable to verify that Sir William Henry Gore was in fact a Baronet of Manor Gore, County Antrim, Ireland. All research regarding the Baronets of Manor Gore point to Sir Paul Gore (of County Donegal, Ireland) and his descendants, of which William Henry Gore is not listed anywhere. It's still possible that William Henry Gore may have been a Baronet and may have been a descendant of one of Sir Paul Gore's children, brothers, or other family members, but unable to find convincing evidence.
