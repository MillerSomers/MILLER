Robert MENDENHALL 1 2

  • Born: Jan 20, 1779, York County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage: Anna HOYLE on Apr 22, 1804 in Lincoln County, North Carolina
  • Died: Apr 4, 1859, Gaston County, North Carolina at age 80

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 5th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
ANCESTRY: Grand-Nephew of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Once Removed) to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Twice Removed) to Thomas Corwin Mendenhall (born 1841), Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake

NOTEWORTHY: Robert was a slave owner
