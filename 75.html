  General Notes:

VOYAGE ON THE "QUEEN MARY": Helene and her husband Arthur took a 3 month vacation to the United Kingdom in 1956. They departed New York on Cunard's "R.M.S. Queen Mary" and arrived in Southampton, England on 13 Aug 1956.
