Leander P. MILLER 1 2 3 4

  • Born: Jan 9, 1849, Cass County, Michigan
  • Marriage (1): Maggie Ellen HOGUE on Aug 14, 1870 in Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan
  • Marriage (2): Mary L. SCHAFER on Nov 6, 1887 in LaGrange, Cass County, Michigan
  • Marriage (3): Marie SIMPSON on Aug 14, 1895 in Jefferson Township, Cass County, Michigan
  • Died: Oct 14, 1895, Jefferson Township, Cass County, Michigan at age 46
  • Buried: Reames-Norton Cemetery, Cassopolis, Cass County, Michigan 5

  General Notes:

RELIGION: Embraced religion at an early age and united with the Free Will Baptist Church at Brownsville. After two years he transferred to the First Christain Church at Jefferson, of which he remained a faithful member until death. He left a wife and three children.
