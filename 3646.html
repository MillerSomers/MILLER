  General Notes:

James Cicero Yancy was born June 15, 1848 in Forsyth, Georgia. Son of Francis Marion Yancey and Mary Frances Fitzpatrick. Husband of Frances "Fanny" Elizabeth Holland. They were married on 07 NOV 1867 in Van Wert, Polk County, Georgia. James died at the age of 45 yrs 10 mos and 23 days of possible malanoma that effected his face.
