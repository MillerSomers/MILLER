Russell FORCE

  • Marriage: Blanche Cora STARN

  General Notes:

DEATH: Russell Force died in an accident in December 1911, one month before his daughter Lucille's birth.

ADOPTION OF CHILDREN: After Russell's untimely death, Blanche's second husband Claude Witwer adopted Russell's two children William and Lucille.

  Research Notes:

Information provided by Russell's grandson - Lucille's son - Bill Palfy in 2013


Russell married Blanche Cora STARN, daughter of William W. STARN and Angeline PLYMESSER. (Blanche Cora STARN was born on Jun 5, 1888 in Smithville, Wayne County, Ohio and died in 1937.)
