Pennell MENDENHALL

  • Born: 22 Apr 1811, Beaver, Beaver County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Mariah WEST on 21 Feb 1833 in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 16 Apr 1871, Ridgeville, Randolph County, Indiana at age 59

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 6th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Uncle of Thomas Corwin Mendenhall, Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake
OCCUPATION: Shoe Cobbler


Pennell married Mariah WEST on 21 Feb 1833 in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. (Mariah WEST was born on 17 May 1811 in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania, died on 11 Oct 1880 in Ridgeville, Randolph County, Indiana and was buried in Ridgeville, Randolph County, Indiana.)
