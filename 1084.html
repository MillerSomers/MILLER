William "Bill" Richard CARTER

  • Born: 26 Aug 1927, Crowder, Choctaw County, Oklahoma
  • Died: 25 Aug 1996, Beaumont, Riverside County, California at age 68
  • Buried: Mountain View Cemetery, Beaumont, Riverside County, California

  General Notes:

NOTES: Bill's mother (Laura Cooper-Carter) died in 1928 when Bill was a young child. Bill's maternal grandfather (David Cooper) then died in 1930. As a result, Bill's father (Roy Carter) and Bill's grandmother (Ruth Eastes-Cooper), moved in together to raise Bill, and continued to live together until Ruth's death in 1964.
