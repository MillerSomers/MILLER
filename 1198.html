Lord John William Maule RAMSEY 13th Earl of Dalhousie

  • Born: 29 Jan 1847, Fraserburgh, Aberdeenshire Council, Scotland
  • Marriage (1): Lady Ida Louisa BENNET on 6 Dec 1877 in Westminster, London, England
  • Died: 25 Nov 1887, Le Havre, France at age 40

  General Notes:

MILITARY: He gained the rank of Commander in the service of the Royal Navy
NOTEWORTHY: He held the offices of Equerry and Extra Equerry to HRH The Duke of Edinburgh;
Member of Parliament;
Held the office of Lord-in-Waiting in Ordinary to HM Queen Victoria;
Succeeded to the title of 13th Earl of Dalhousie in 1880;
Succeeded to the title of 2nd Baron Ramsay of Glenmark in 1880;
Succeeded to the title of 14th Lord Ramsay of Dalhousie in 1880;
Succeeded to the title of 13th Lord Ramsay of Carrington in 1880;
Invested as a Knight, Order of the Thistle in 1881;
Held office of Secretary of State for Scotland in 1886
DEATH: His wife died of peritonitis (abdominal infection) at the age of 30 while in France after they returned from a trip to the United States. He was so distraught that he died of a stroke the next morning at age of 40.

(Lady Ida Louisa BENNET was born on 22 Jun 1857 in London, England and died on 24 Nov 1887 in Le Havre, France.)
