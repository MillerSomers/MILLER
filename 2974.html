James Clayton "Clay" RAULSTON 1

  • Born: Aug 28, 1928, Sadler, Grayson County, Texas
  • Died: Dec 1, 1998, Salem, Marion County, Oregon at age 70
  • Buried: Eternal Hills Cemetery, Klamath Falls, Klamath County, Oregon 2

  General Notes:

James Clayton Raulston, 70, a former resident of Klamath Falls and Chiloquin, died Dec. 1, 1998, in Salem. Mr. Raulston was born in Texas. He lived in Klamath Falls from 1939 until moving to Aurora in 1986. In 1994, he moved to Chiloquin and, in 1997, to Salem. He was a sawmill worker. He is survived by his sister, Judy Daudt of Canby.
