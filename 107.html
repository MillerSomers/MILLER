  General Notes:

NAME: Benjamin Summerfield Ramsey
MILITARY: Civil War; Volunteer, enlisted 2 May 1864;
-- Company F, 131st Ohio Infantry (National Guard), from 14 May 1864 (Dayton, Ohio) to 25 Aug 1864 (Camp Chase, Ohio), rank Corporal;
-- Company H, 197th Ohio Infantry, from 3 Mar 1865 (Dayton, Ohio) to 31 Jul 1865 (Baltimore, Maryland), promoted to Full Sergeant on 10 Apr 1865
OCCUPATIONS: Farm Insurance Agent (1880 U.S. Census);
Cigar Maker (1900 U.S. Census)
RESIDENCES: North Manchester, Wabash County, IN (1880 U.S. Census)
908 South 8th Street, Goshen, Elkhart County, IN (1900 U.S. Census)
RELIGION: Protestant
DEATH: Had to occur after 4 Feb 1909 (see Veterans Home records)
NOTE: For research purposes, do not confuse this Benjamin Ramsey with another Benjamin Ramsey also born in Ohio (1844) who also served in the Civil War, but ended up blind and died in 1926 in Missouri
