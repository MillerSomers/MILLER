Bessie Christine JOHNSON

  • Born: 9 Feb 1869, Hobart, Lake County, Indiana
  • Died: 14 Aug 1941, Los Angeles County, California at age 72
  • Buried: Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Glendale, Los Angeles County, California

  General Notes:

OCCUPATION: Stenographer (as per the 1900 U.S. Census)
PASSPORT: Bessie applied for a U.S. passport in 1919. This was after she had already lived in Mexico City from 1906-1917 with her sister and brother-in-law Pauline & Thomas Gore at Thomas Gore's Hotel Geneve.
MARRIAGE: According to her 1919 Passport Application, she had never been married as of 1919.
