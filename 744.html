Frances Marian WYNN

  • Born: 27 Nov 1920, Crowder, Pittsburg County, Oklahoma
  • Marriage (1): Robert Henry SMITH on 20 Jun 1938 in Yuma County, Arizona
  • Died: 2 Sep 2001, Yuma, Yuma County, Arizona at age 80
  • Buried: 7 Sep 2001, Desert Lawn Memorial Park, Yuma, Yuma County, Arizona

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Welsh King Rhodri ap Mawr "Rhodri the Great", who reined from 844-878
ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 10th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
ANCESTRY: 5th Great-Niece of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Six Times Removed) to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
ANCESTRY: Third-Cousin (Thrice Removed) to Thomas Corwin Mendenhall (born 1841), Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake

RESIDENCES: Frances moved from Oklahoma to Somerton, Arizona as a teenager in 1936;
In 1938, she married Robert Smith and they settled on a farm near Gadsden where they lived for 40 years
ACTIVITIES: She was active for many years as a leader in the Gadsden 4-H Club and in the Gadsden Homemakers Club
