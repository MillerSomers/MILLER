Adam CLAPP

  • Born: 10 Dec 1754, Orange County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Emma Ruth MARLEY in 1780 in Guilford County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (2): Ruth LAWRENCE in 1794 in Robertson County, Tennessee
  • Died: 15 Aug 1841, Alexander, Illinois at age 86

  General Notes:

MARRIAGE: Some family trees list his marriage to Ruth Lawrence as occurring in North Carolina. This is doubtful as he had already moved to Tennessee with his first wife Emma Marley and had already borne 2 children in Tennessee. Plus, all of the children of Adam and Ruth were borne in Tennessee. So it seems more likely that their marriage occurred in Tennessee.


Adam married Emma Ruth MARLEY in 1780 in Guilford County, North Carolina. (Emma Ruth MARLEY was born in 1760 in Delaware and died in 1798 in Tennessee.)


Adam next married Ruth LAWRENCE in 1780 in Guilford County, North Carolina. (Ruth LAWRENCE was born in 1764 in Guilford County, North Carolina and died in 1847 in Alexander, Illinois.)
