OBITUARY:
Michael Lewis Wilson
October 8, 1949 to December 9, 2020

Michael Lewis Wilson, age 71 of Chatsworth, CA passed away peacefully on December 9, 2020. He had been battling heart and kidney problems for many years.

Michael was born in Yuma and grew up in Somerton. He is a 1967 graduate of Yuma High. Michael had worked as a model, and in the catering and restaurant industry. After retiring, Michael enjoyed gardening, cooking, and spending time and traveling with his life partner of 34 years.

Family members preceding him in death were older brother Dave Richard Wilson, and younger half-brother Kenneth "Ray" Raulston, who both perished in a small plane crash in 1971 in Somerton. Also parents Lewis Chalmers Wilson and Ada "Jane" Wynn-Wilson-Raulston.

Michael is survived by his life partner Glenn Miller, and half-sister Virginia Raulston.

You were the most loving, joyous, and giving spirit I've ever known. You will forever be in my heart, I will miss you always.

(posted in Yuma Sun 12-12-2020)
