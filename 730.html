Ruth Newel EASTES 1 2 3 4

  • Born: May 31, 1881, Alma, Crawford County, Arkansas
  • Marriage: Dave Richard "Sam" COOPER on Sep 11, 1895 in Canadian, Choctaw Land (Pittsburg County), Oklahoma
  • Died: Jul 25, 1964, Beaumont, Riverside County, California at age 83 5
  • Buried: Mountain View Cemetery, Beaumont, Riverside County, California 6

  General Notes:

NOTES: Ruth's daughter (Laura Cooper-Carter) died in 1928 when Laura's son Bill was a young child. Ruth's husband (David Cooper) then died in 1930. As a result, Ruth and her son-in-law (Roy Carter) moved in together to raise Bill, and continued to live together until Ruth's death in 1964.

DATE OF DEATH: According to the California Death Index, Ruth died on 25 July 1964, NOT 20 July 1964 (as stated on her Find A Grave Memorial #45276765). Her headstone only listed the year of her death (1964).
