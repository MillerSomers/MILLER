Hazel GWYN

  • Born: Jul 17, 1927, Concord, Cabarrus County, North Carolina
  • Marriage: Dolan Hoover SMITH
  • Died: Sep 27, 2013, Concord, Cabarrus County, North Carolina at age 86
  • Buried: Oakwood Cemetery, Concord, Cabarrus County, North Carolina 1

  General Notes:

Hazel Smith was a daughter of the late George R. and Annie Clark Gwyn.
She graduated from Concord High School.
OCCUPATION: During World War II she worked with the Civil Service. For more than four decades she owned Wil-Mar Clothing.
CHURCH: She was a life long member of Epworth United Methodist Church.
SPOUSE: Her husband of 59 years, "Hoover" Smith preceded her in death in 2009.
CHILDREN: She was the mother to a son, Guy Hoover Smith and his wife Carla; a daughter, Connie Smith Rogers and husband, John. There are seven grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
