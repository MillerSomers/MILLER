  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Milton's father was born in Russia, his mother was born in New York

1936 TRIP: When Milton was 17, he and his parents took a trip from Los Angeles to New York (through the Panama Canal with a stop at Havana, Cuba), on the S.S. President Taft (leaving Los Angeles on 28 Jun 1936 and arriving in New York on 13 Jul 1936). Their home address at that time was 217 So. Crescent Drive, Beverly Hills, California.

MILITARY: Private, U.S. Army, 307th Infantry Regiment, 77th Infantry Division, World War II, killed in action in 1945 in Okinawa, Japan (Service # 39591195);
Milton was awarded the Bronze Star Medal and the Purple Heart (World War II Honor Roll)

BURIAL: Milton's remains were interred 4 years after his death on 27 Oct 1949 at the National Memorial Cemetery of The Pacific Cemetery;
Address: 2177 Puowaina Drive Honolulu, HI 96813;
Buried at: Section Q, Plot A, Row 0, Site 999
