Jacob FAUST

  • Born: 25 Dec 1757, Bern Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania

  • Died: 27 Jul 1834, Orwigsburg, Schuylkill County, Pennsylvania at age 76

  General Notes:

NOTES:
Son of Philip Faust and Mateline Albrecht. Husband of Elizabeth Kieffer m. 22 OCT 1782 Berks Co, PA.

Zions (Red) Church Cemetery, Orwigsburg, Berks County
(Now Schuylkill County), Pa.

In Memory of Those Revolutionary Soldiers Buried
in Zion's (Red) Church Cemetery:
Jacob Faust in Capt. Ladich's Co.

Berks Co., PA

Jacob & Elizabeth Faust had 14 Children:

Inscription:
Hier ruhet Jacob Faust - Er war geboren den 25 December 1757 - Lebte mit Elisabeth geborne Keiffer 52 Jahre im .... Ehestand - zeugte mit ihr 11 kinder und starb am 27 Juli 1844 - in den Alter von 76 Jahren 7 Monaten und 2 Tagen

English Translation: Here rests Jacob Faust - He was born the 25 December 1757 - Lived with Elizabeth nee Keiffer 52 years .... in matrimony - her begot 11 children, and died on 27 July 1844 - in the age of 76 years 7 months and 2 days
