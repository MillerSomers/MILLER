  • Marriage: Helena GUMM in 1892 in Wisconsin 1

   Another name for Nels was Nels HANSON.

  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION: Denmark to U.S.A. in 1880 according to 1900 and 1910 U.S. Census

EMPLOYMENT: Engineer at a Sash and Door Shop (1910 U.S. Census)

NOTE: This is NOT the same Niels "Nels" Hansen who was born in Denmark in 1845 and migrated to the U.S. in 1864 and whose spouse was Mathilda Lovisa Olson-Hansen

NAME: There is some confusion whether his last name was HANSEN or HANSON. The 1900 and 1910 U.S. Census show HANSON, but his daughter Lydia's (Dorothy) 1901 birth certificate and 1919 marriage certificate shows HANSEN. Also, Dorothy's son Glenn's birth certificate shows HANSEN.
