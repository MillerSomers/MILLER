Nancy

  • Born: 1842, Tennessee
  • Marriage (1): John Samuel FREEMAN
  • Died: 1881, Rea Valley, Marion County, Arkansas at age 39

  General Notes:

Nancy's maiden name is unknown. She first married Henry Cypert and had 2 daughters, Safrona and Henrietta. Henry died 1863 in AR during the Civil War. Nancy married John Samuel Freeman in 1864. Their children were Nancy Jane, Mary Rebecca, Martha Elizabeth, John David, Moses Canada, and Louellen.

(courtesy of Find A Grave, Memorial # 142084148)
