Adam MENDENHALL 1

  • Born: Oct 29, 1768, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Eliza ALLEN on Mar 25, 1794 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (2): Amelia BROADHURST
  • Died: Jan 22, 1835, Delaware County, Pennsylvania at age 66
  • Buried: Stark County, Ohio

  General Notes:

RELIGION: Adam was disowned by the Quakers 15 Oct 1794 at their Wilmington, DE, Monthly Meeting for "manifesting a departure from our testimony in dress and address and has also accomplished his marriage to a woman in membership by the assistance of a hireling priest.
MARRIAGES: Married Amelia Broadhurst two years after his wife Eliza died


Adam married Eliza ALLEN on Mar 25, 1794 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. (Eliza ALLEN was born in 1772 and died on Oct 19, 1798 in Chester County, Pennsylvania.)


Adam next married Amelia BROADHURST. (Amelia BROADHURST was born about 1777 and died on Feb 18, 1821.)
