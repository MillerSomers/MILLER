Adam MENDENHALL

  • Born: 29 Oct 1768, Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Eliza ALLEN on 25 Mar 1794 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (2): Amelia BROADHURST
  • Died: 22 Jan 1835, Delaware County, Pennsylvania at age 66
  • Buried: Stark County, Ohio

  General Notes:

RELIGION: Adam was disowned by the Quakers 15 Oct 1794 at their Wilmington, DE, Monthly Meeting for "manifesting a departure from our testimony in dress and address and has also accomplished his marriage to a woman in membership by the assistance of a hireling priest.
MARRIAGES: Married Amelia Broadhurst two years after his wife Eliza died


Adam married Eliza ALLEN on 25 Mar 1794 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. (Eliza ALLEN was born in 1772 and died on 19 Oct 1798 in Chester County, Pennsylvania.)


Adam next married Amelia BROADHURST on 25 Mar 1794 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. (Amelia BROADHURST was born about 1777 and died on 18 Feb 1821.)
