   Another name for Evelyn was Minerva Evelyn HOLLAND.

  General Notes:

NAME: Son Lewis Wilson wrote his mother's name as "Evelyn Minerva Holland"
DEATH: She died at the age of 25 of Tuberculosis (as did her husband 15 years later). The only record found of her death is on page 94 of the Hudson Funeral Home Records in Oklahoma. Also, Evelyn's granddaughter Mary Molesworth recalls that Evelyn died when her mother Nadine was 6 weeks old in 1924. Also, Evelyn's grandson Michael Wilson recalls that Evelyn died when his father Lewis was 8 years old in 1924. Also, Evelyn's husband Forest was already listed as a widower on the 1930 U.S. Census 5
