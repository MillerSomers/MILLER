Catherine CONDUITT

  • Born: 23 May 1721, England
  • Marriage (1): Hon. John WALLOP Viscount Lymington on 12 Jul 1740 in England
  • Buried: 4 May 1750, Westminster Abbey, London, England

  General Notes:

FAMILY: Great-Niece of Sir Isaac Newton
NOTEWORTHY: She became Viscountess Lymington when her husband John succeeded to the title
BURIAL: She is buried alongside her parents at Westminster Abbey
