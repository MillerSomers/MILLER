Margaret MENDENHALL 1

  • Born: Dec 9, 1800, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage: William GRIFFITH on May 10, 1821 in West Chester, Chester County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: Aug 28, 1824, West Chester, Chester County, Pennsylvania at age 23

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 6th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Aunt of Thomas Corwin Mendenhall, Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake
DEATH: Died as a result of Dropsy (Edema)


Margaret married William GRIFFITH on May 10, 1821 in West Chester, Chester County, Pennsylvania. (William GRIFFITH was born about 1801.)
