Thomas MILDENHALL III

  • Born: 1630, Mildenhall, Ramsbury, Wiltshire County, England
  • Marriage (1): Joane STROUD on 12 Aug 1649 in Aldbourne, Wiltshire County, England
  • Marriage (2): Joan STRODE
  • Died: 30 Jun 1682, Somerset House, London, England at age 52
  • Buried: 5 Jul 1682

   Another name for Thomas was Thomas MENDENHALL.

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
FAMILY: Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
FAMILY: Great-Grandfather of William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
RELIGION: Quaker
NAME CHANGE: Several of his children migrated to America and changed their name to "Mendenhall"
MARRIAGE: Much more likely that JOANE STROUD was his wife, not Joan Strode (see notes for Joan Strode)
