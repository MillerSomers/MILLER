Elizabeth married John HATTON.


Elizabeth next married Robert MENDENHALL, son of Rev. Benjamin MENDENHALL and Ann A. PENNELL, on 13 Nov 1734 in Birmingham Township, Chester County, Pennsylvania. (Robert MENDENHALL was born on 7 Sep 1713 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania, died on 23 Jun 1785 in Concord, Chester County, Pennsylvania and was buried in Delaware County, Pennsylvania.)
