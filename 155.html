Mary Alice WHITT

  • Born: 5 Feb 1853, Rockingham County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Jacob "Frank" Franklin SOMERS Jr. on 4 Nov 1873 in Rockingham County, North Carolina
  • Died: 30 Nov 1929, Williamsburg, Rockingham County, North Carolina at age 76
  • Buried: Camp Springs United Methodist Church Cemetery, Caswell County, NC

  General Notes:

DEATH: Mary's North Carolina Death Certificate clearly states she died at 1:00 pm on November 30, 1929. However, her gravestone shows her date of death as December 1, 1929, which is incorrect.
