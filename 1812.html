Barbara Marie CLAPP

  • Born: 1737, Guilford County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): John Phillip CLAPP on 18 Aug 1751 in Oley Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania
  • Died: 11 Apr 1821, Whitsett, Guilford County, North Carolina at age 84

  General Notes:

MARRIAGE: She married her first cousin John Phillip Clapp, both having the same grandfather (Johann Jost Klapp)
DUPLICATE: There appears to be another Barbara Marie Clapp (aka Barbary Clapp) who married a Daniel May. These two women named Barbara Marie Clapp have somehow been merged into one entry on many family trees and as having the same parents, and listing Barbara Marie Clapp with two simultaneous marriages (one in Pennsylvania and the other in North Carolina) and giving birth to 19 children in two states.
