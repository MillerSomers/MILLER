Asa Chester GLIDDEN

  • Born: 24 Oct 1867, Van Buren County, Michigan
  • Marriage (1): Bettie M. FRANKLIN on 26 Apr 1909
  • Died: Jan 1933, La Porte County, Indiana at age 65
  • Buried: Lakeside Cemetery, Berrien County, Michigan

  General Notes:

ASA CHESTER GLIDDEN was a member of the firm of Glidden Brothers, general merchants at Lakeside. He was born in Porter township, Van Buren County. His parents were Stephen M. and Harriett L. Glidden, natives of New York. Asa C. Glidden who was a member of the firm of Glidden Brothers and was in charge of the grocery and meat market owned by the firm at Michigan City.

Asa married Bettie M. Franklin on April 26, 1909. They didn't have any children.
