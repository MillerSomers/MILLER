  General Notes:

OBITUARY:
Berlin, Wisconsin - ARTHUR F. HANSEN, 62, operator of a grocery store and apartment building here for the past 25 years, died suddenly Tuesday while visiting at Clam Lake. Death was attributed to a heart attack.

He was born in Racine on July 4, 1894 [sic], son of the late Nels and Helen Hansen. He was married in Waukegan, Ill., in 1929 to Margaret Mae MacCarthy. The couple moved to Berlin 25 years ago and have been in business here since.

Surviving are his widow, two sisters, and several nieces and nephews.
(Oshkosh Daily Northwestern (WI), 3 Oct 1956)
