  General Notes:

VIOLA MAE MORLEY-HELTSLEY
Oct. 16, 2008

TITUSVILLE, FL - Viola Mae Heltsley, a loving mother, wife and friend, departed this life on October 16, 2008, at Royal Oaks Nursing and Rehab. She was born in Lakeside, Michigan, to John and Clare Morley. Viola came to Titusville from Clearwater, FL in 2000. Viola was a member of First United Methodist Church of Pinellas Park, FL.

She was preceded in death by her first husband of 26 years, Robert Bruce Hammons, and her second husband of 15 years, Jim Roberts.

Viola is survived by her daughter Michelle Fischer and her husband Pat of Titusville, and her grandchildren, Kailey and Ryan Fischer. A graveside service will be held Saturday, November 8, 2008 at Lakeside Cemetery, Lakeside, Michigan. Please sign Viola's guestbook at www.northbrevardfuneralhome.com .

Published in the South Bend Tribune on 11/2/2008
