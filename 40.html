William GORE 1 2

  • Born: Mar 22, 1838, Fedney, County Down, Ireland
  • Marriage: Dorothea Henrietta Waller CRAMPTON on Sep 13, 1860
  • Died: May 18, 1886, Wicklow County, Ireland at age 48

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: J.P. for County Fermanagh; formerly Lieutenant 13th Hussars
WILL: His Will was probated on 6 Aug 1886...
"Probate of the Will of William Gore formerly of Innismore Hall near Enniskillen in the County of Fermanagh and late of Brennans-terrace Bray in the County of Wicklow J.P. who died on or about 18 May 1886 at Brennans-terrace granted 3 July 1886 at Dublin to Dorothea Henrietta Gore of Brennans-terrace, Widow one of the Executors."

  Research Notes:

-- "A Genealogical and Heraldic History of the Landed Gentry of Ireland" by Sir Bernard Burke, C.B., LL.D., Ulster King of Arms, page 174
-- England & Wales, National Probate Calendar (Index of Wills and Administrations), 1858-1966
-- Scots, Irish, English and a few Welsh ancestors (http://wc.rootsweb.ancestry.com/cgi-bin/igm.cgi?op=SHOW&db=jenniemacfie)
