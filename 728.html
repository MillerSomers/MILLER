Elizabeth May PARKS

  • Born: 25 Jan 1871, Central City, Sebastian County, Arkansas
  • Marriage (1): John Austin WYNN on 1 Sep 1891 in Canadian, Choctaw Land (Pittsburg County), Oklahoma
  • Died: 2 Apr 1902, Pittsburg County, Arkansas at age 31
  • Buried: Massey Cemetery, Crowder, Pittsburg County, Oklahoma

  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 8th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
ANCESTRY: 3rd Great-Niece of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Four Times Removed) to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
ANCESTRY: Third-Cousin (Once Removed) to Thomas Corwin Mendenhall (born 1841), Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake
