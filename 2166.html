Carl Edwin BOSTWICK Sr.

  • Born: 10 Jun 1879, Elkhart, Elkhart County, Indiana
  • Marriage (1): Winifred S. SWART on 31 Oct 1907 in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois
  • Marriage (2): Zanaida G.
  • Died: 23 Feb 1944, Milwaukee, Milwaukee County, Wisconsin at age 64

  General Notes:

OCCUPATION: Manager at printing company (1920 U.S. Census)
RESIDENCE: Milwaukee, Wisconsin (1920 U.S. Census)
DIVORCE: Carl and Winifred were divorced by the time the 1930 U.S. Census was taken
DEATH DISCREPANCY: According to the 1940 U.S. Census in Los Angeles, his wife Winifred S. Swart-Bostwick clearly listed her marital status as a "widow". But there is also a Carl E. Bostwick listed on the 1940 U.S. Census in Milwaukee who was born in 1880 in Indiana, working in Milwaukee as a manager at a printing company, but with a new wife Zanaida (born about 1897 in Illinois). The chances of their being two Carl E. Bostwicks born in the same place, same year, working in the same industry, and in the same occupation in the same city is highly unlikely. I'm inclined to believe Carl was still alive in 1940, that Carl and Winifred were divorced by then, and that Winifred listed her marital status incorrectly.
