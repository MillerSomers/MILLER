LaMar Silverthorne MILLER

  • Born: 28 May 1895, Goshen, Elkhart County, Indiana
  • Marriage (1): Lydia "Dorothy" Amelia HANSEN on 2 May 1919 in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois
  • Died: 13 May 1964, Glendale, Los Angeles County, California at age 68
  • Buried: 20 May 1964, Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Glendale, Los Angeles County, California

   Cause of his death was Arterosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease.

  General Notes:

OCCUPATIONS: Professional Magician;
Engraver (1920 U.S. Census);
Coffee Salesman (1930 U.S. Census);
Marketing Salesman (1930's-1940's);
Security at Lockheed Aircraft Corp. in Burbank, California

RESIDENCES: Elkhart County, IN (1895)
Chicago, Cook County, IL (1904-1930);
Los Angeles County, CA (1931-1940's);
St. Helena, Napa County, CA (1942);
Los Angeles County, CA (1946);
Mexico City, D.F., Mexico (1960);
Glendale, Los Angeles County, CA (1964)

DEATH: Arterosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease

BURIAL: LaMar was cremated on May 16, 1964. On May 20, 1964, he was placed in Niche 29006 in the Iris Columbarium at Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Glendale. When his wife Dorothy (Lydia) died 13 years later in 1977, their son (Glenn Dale Miller) had LaMar's remains moved next to Dorothy's cremated remains in Niche 35979 in the Columbarium of Understanding, Freedom Mausoleum at Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Glendale.
