Lydia "Dorothy" Amelia HANSEN

  • Born: 1 May 1901, Racine, Racine County, Wisconsin
  • Marriage (1): LaMar Silverthorne MILLER on 2 May 1919 in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois
  • Died: 22 Sep 1977, Glendale, Los Angeles County, California at age 76
  • Buried: 24 Sep 1977, Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Glendale, Los Angeles County, California

   Cause of her death was Congestive Heart Failure, Arteriosclerotic Heart Disease, and Arteriosclerosis.

   Another name for Lydia was Lydia Amelia HANSON.

  General Notes:

NAME: There is some confusion whether her maiden name was HANSEN or HANSON. Her marriage record indicates HANSEN, but her birth record as well as the 1900 and 1910 U.S. Census indicate HANSON. Her son Glenn's birth and death certificates indicate its HANSEN.

RESIDENCES: Chicago, Cook County, IL (1930 U.S. Census);
Los Angeles County, CA (1931-1940's);
St. Helena, Napa County, CA (1942);
Los Angeles County, CA (1946);
Mexico City, D.F., Mexico (1960);
Glendale, Los Angeles County, CA (1964-1977)

DEATH: Congestive Heart Failure

BURIAL: Niche 35979, Columbarium of Understanding, Freedom Mausoleum at the Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Glendale, Los Angeles, CA

NOTE: Do not confuse this Lydia Hansen with another Lydia Hansen (born 26 Jan 1901 in Colfax, Wisconsin and died 13 Jul 1992) who's husband was a Verner Daniel Larsen. Also, do not confuse this Lydia "Dorothy" Amelia Hansen with another Dorothy Amelia Hanson (born 30 May 1901 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin).
