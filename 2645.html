Lavinia SUMMERS

  • Born: 12 Nov 1819, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Jacob FOUST on 9 Mar 1840 in Guilford County, North Carolina
  • Died: 25 Jan 1910 at age 90
  • Buried: Friedens Lutheran Church Cemetery, Gibsonville, Guilford County, NC

   Other names for Lavinia were Levina SUMMERS and Livinia SUMMERS.

  General Notes:

NOTES: 90 years of age at time of death. Daughter of L.W. & Elizabeth Summers, who were of German ancestry, owned a good amount of property, and had several grist mills. Lavinia married Jacob Foust, who was a farmer, and also ran a grist mill. She was the mother of one daughter, Mary, who married Joseph Bason Whitsett. One of her grandchildren was William Thornton Whitsett, who became the principal of the Whitsett Institute.
