Anna Barbara GOSSAUER VON REISBACH

  • Born: 25 Jul 1697, Langenselbold, Main-Kinzig-Kreis, Hessen, Germany
  • Marriage (1): Johannes Jacob ALBRECHT about 1715 in Langenselbold, Hesse-Darmstadt, Germany
  • Died: 1765, Bern Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania at age 68
  • Buried: Fritz Burial Grounds, Berks County, Pennsylvania

  General Notes:

IMMIGRATION:
Records of the Port of Philadelphia list Johannes Albrecht and his wife Anna Barbara (maiden name not listed) as Palatine passengers of the "Johnson Galley" which arrived from Rotterdam on September 18, 1732.

The ages of the five children on arrival were: Matelina-16 (she was mistakenly listed as "under 14"), Barbara-13, Christian-6, Jacob-5, and Lodawick-10 months who was born November 11, 1731 in Ruttenberg, Germany.

  Research Notes:

Find A Grave, Memorial # 96800216


Anna married Johannes Jacob ALBRECHT, son of Hendrich Von Oberengstringen ALBRECHT and Anna Elizabeth FOLSOM, about 1715 in Langenselbold, Hesse-Darmstadt, Germany. (Johannes Jacob ALBRECHT was born on 19 Dec 1697 in Pfalz, Hessen, Bayern, Germany, died in 1752 in Little Oley Township, Berks County, Pennsylvania and was buried in Fritz Burial Grounds, Berks County, Pennsylvania.)
