Lord Charles Augustus BENNET 5th Earl of Tankerville

  • Born: 28 Apr 1776, England
  • Marriage (1): Armandine Sophie Leonie Corisande DE GRAMONT Countess of Tankerville on 28 Jul 1806
  • Died: 25 Jun 1859 at age 83

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: Titled "Lord Tankerville";
He was Lord Ossulston until 1822 when he succeeded his father in the earldom becoming the 5th Earl of Tankerville;
Served as the British Treasury of the Household from 1806-1807 in the Ministry of All the Talents;
Member of Parliament for three terms between 1803 and 1822
