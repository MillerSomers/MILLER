  General Notes:

NOTES:
His father-in-law was "Red Eagle" William Weatherford, Chief of the Wind Clan (Hutalgalgi) of the Muscogee (Creek) Nation, who famously battled and then surrendered to Colonel Andrew Jackson at Fort Toulouse (Fort Jackson) during the Creek War.


William married Mary Levitia WEATHERFORD, daughter of General William "Billy" (Chief Red Eagle) WEATHERFORD, Sr. and Mary STIGGINS, on Apr 11, 1842 in Monroe County, Alabama. (Mary Levitia WEATHERFORD was born on Mar 17, 1823 in Little River, Baldwin County, Alabama, died on Jun 21, 1859 in Pleasant Hill, Sabine Parish, Louisana and was buried in Old Camp Ground Cemetery, Old Pleasant Hill, De Soto Parish, Louisana 4.)
