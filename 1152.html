Hon. John WALLOP 1st Earl of Portsmouth

  • Born: 15 Apr 1690, England
  • Marriage (1): Lady Bridget BENNET on 20 May 1716 in England
  • Died: 22 Nov 1762 at age 72

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: He was a British Peer and Member of Parliament;
In 1720 he became the 1st Viscount Lymington (above a Baron, but below an Earl or Count) of Lymington, Hampshire, England;
In 1743 he became the Earl of Portsmouth;
In 1743 he became the Governor of the Isle of Wight;
Was appointed Junior Lord of the Treasury of Great Britain in 1717
