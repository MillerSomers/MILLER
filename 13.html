  General Notes:

ANCESTRY: Descended from Welsh King Rhodri ap Mawr "Rhodri the Great", who reined from 844-878
ANCESTRY: Descended from Warinus de la Strode (born 1020 in Bretagne, France), a French knight who lived at the time of William the Conqueror
ANCESTRY: 10th Great-Grandchild of John Mildenhall Jr. (born 1560), the only Englishmen to visit both royal courts of the Emperors Akbar and Jahangir, and the first Englishmen to be buried in India
ANCESTRY: 5th Great-Niece of John Bartram, the "Father of American Botany"
ANCESTRY: First-Cousin (Six Times Removed) to William Bartram, "America's first Ornithologist"
ANCESTRY: Third-Cousin (Thrice Removed) to Thomas Corwin Mendenhall (born 1841), Alaska's Mendenhall Glacier's namesake

OCCUPATION: Seamstress and Upholsterer
MARRIAGE: Jane married her second husband Kenneth Raulston about 1952 but then divorced him. She then remarried Kenneth in 1963 in Imperial, California
DEATH: Lung Cancer

Ada married Lewis Chalmers WILSON, son of Forest "Shorty" Greeley WILSON and Evelyn "Eva" Minerva HOLLAND, on Aug 1, 1946 in Yuma County, Arizona. The marriage ended in divorce. (Lewis Chalmers WILSON was born on Aug 20, 1916 in Wetumka, Hughes County, Oklahoma,4 died on Feb 2, 1989 in Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona and was buried on Feb 9, 1989 in National Memorial Cemetery, Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona 5.) The cause of his death was Massive Benign Brain Tumor.


Ada next married Kenneth Clyde RAULSTON, son of William Delbert RAULSTON and Mary Tennessee HACKWORTH, on Jun 1, 1952 in Kern County, California. (Kenneth Clyde RAULSTON was born on Feb 7, 1919 in Sadler, Grayson County, Texas, died on Jan 27, 1977 in Yuma, Yuma County, Arizona 6 and was buried in Desert Lawn Memorial Park, Yuma, Yuma County, Arizona 7.) The cause of his death was Cancer.


Ada next married Edward SMITH in 1977-1978. The marriage ended in divorce in 1980. (Edward SMITH was born on Apr 5, 1923 in Gadsden, Yuma County, Arizona and died on Feb 14, 2012 in Visalia, Tulare County, California.)
