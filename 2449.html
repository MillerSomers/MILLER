Milton John MORLEY 1 2 3

  • Born: Apr 29, 1835, Harrisburg, Dauphin County, Pennsylvania
  • Marriage (1): Orpha BIXBY
  • Marriage (2): Amanda S. "Mandia" HANOVER on Jan 23, 1875 in Weesaw, Berrien County, Michigan
  • Died: Dec 6, 1916, Weesaw, Berrien County, Michigan at age 81
  • Buried: Galien Township Cemetery, Berrien County, Michigan 4

  General Notes:

Wife Amanda and their Children - Milton John Morley had a second wife - Amanda S "Mandia" Hanover, daughter of John C HANOVER and Caroline WELLS. They were married 23 Jan 1875 in Weesaw Twp, Berrien County, Michigan. Amanda was born ca. 1855 in Berrien County, Michigan; died ca. 1937 in Galien, Berrien County, Michigan. They had seven children -
Alta, d. 31 Dec 1875;
unnamed son, b&d 3 May 1881;
Clara M b. 4 Nov 1882 Weesaw Twp, Berrien County, Michigan, on census 16 Jun 1900;
John Sherman, b. 20 Jan 1888 Weesaw Twp, Berrien County, Michigan, on census in 1900;
Elvira B "Vira" b. 13 Apr 1891 Berrien County, Michigan; d. 8 Apr 1941 in California; m. 24 Nov 1910 at Weesaw Twp, Berrien County, Michigan to Charles Leo Metzger;
Silvia, b. Sep 1893, on census 16 Jun 1900 and 4 May 1910 both Weesaw Twp, Berrien County, Michigan;
Bertha M. Morley, b. Oct 1899 in Michigan, on census 1900 and 1910.
