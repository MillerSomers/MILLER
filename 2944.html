Lena Estelle SAUNDERS

  • Born: 1 Mar 1916
  • Died: 19 Oct 2010 at age 94
  • Buried: Gilliam's Primitive Baptist Church, Elon College, Alamance County, NC

  General Notes:

Lena Saunders Page was born in Caswell County and was the daughter of George Lee Saunders and Minnie Gwynn. She was a homemaker and attended the Gilliam Primitive Baptist Church. She was a member of the Cherry Grove Senior Citizens Club. She loved her family and especially enjoyed the grandchildren. Even though arthritis plagued her over the last few years, she never complained. Mrs. Page was the picture of strength and courage. She loved her flowers and people always noticed the beautiful display around her home. Along with her parents, she was also preceded in death by her husband, Loften Page and her brother, Scott Saunders.

Mrs. Page is survived by her sons, Wilbert Larry Page, Cecil Lee Page and his wife, Mitzy all of Elon; her daughter, Barbara Page Mize and her husband, Larry of Graham; her grandchildren, Lorrie Varner and her husband, Chet, Teresa Page, Tanya Chu and her husband, Allen, Larry Page and his wife, Michele, Chris Mize and his wife, Stephanie and Beth Terry and her husband, J.D.; 12 great grandchildren; her sisters, Violet Paschal of Burlington and Eunice Kernodle and husband, Staley of Elon; and numerous nieces and nephews. The family will receive friends on Thursday, October 21, 2010 from 6-8 pm. at Wilkerson Funeral Home and other times at her residence.

Source: Wilkerson Funeral Home, Reidsville, North Carolina
