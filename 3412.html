John Samuel FREEMAN

  • Born: Sep 27, 1813, Chocowinity, Beaufort County, North Carolina
  • Marriage (1): Nancy
  • Marriage (2): Annis HARDING
  • Died: Feb 21, 1897, Rea Valley, Marion County, Arkansas at age 83

  General Notes:

John was the son of Samuel and Mary Freeman. He was first married to Annis Harding and they had 9 children. He married second to Nancy Cypert and they had 6 children. His death was listed in The Mountain Echo on Friday, Feb 26, 1897. The notice stated that he lived near the mouth of Crooked Creek, since about 1870. One source indicates he was buried at Freeman Cemetery in Stone County. Another source indicates he was buried in Rea Valley, Marion County, Arkansas.

(courtesy of Find A Grave, Memorial # 142061600)
