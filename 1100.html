Hon. John WALLOP 2nd Earl of Portsmouth

  • Born: 29 Jun 1742, England
  • Marriage (1): Urania FELLOWES on 27 Aug 1763 in England
  • Died: 16 May 1797, England at age 54

  General Notes:

NOTEWORTHY: John was a British nobleman and became Viscount Lymington (above a Baron, but below an Earl or Count) of Lymington, Hampshire, England in 1749;
His father died in 1749, so it was not until 1762 when his grandfather died that he inherited the earldom becoming the 2nd Earl of Portsmouth;
In 1755, he became a Doctor of Civil Law at Oxford University
